The Happenings

This week we had Symantec come in and discuss their line of work and a little bit about their history and different products they offer. Although Symantec is famously known for their product "Norton Anti-Virus", Symantec actually has other data protection tools that are used by a lot of big companies. They also took resumes at the last meeting, so hopefully some of us will be getting calls!
