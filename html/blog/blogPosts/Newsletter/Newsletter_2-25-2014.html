UST Plays Pokemon

If you've been on the internet recently, you've most likely heard about Twitch Plays Pokemon. For those who haven't heard, Twitch Plays Pokemon is a live stream of an emulator running Pokemon Red. What makes this stream unique, is that the viewers can actually cooperatively play the game through the group chat. Wouldn't it be cool if we had our own? Well Andrew Schleicher did just that. Thanks to Andrew, we will be showcasing UST Plays Pokemon this Friday at the LAN Party! We will be able to cooperatively play Pokemon Fire Red on the projector (granted that we can configure the server properly for the stream). For those of you interested in how he created it, he will be writing a blog post about the process soon; so stay tuned!
