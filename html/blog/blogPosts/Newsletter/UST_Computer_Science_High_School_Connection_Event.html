UST Computer Science High School Connection Event

by Amanda Maricle-Roberts

Posted on April 7, 2014 at 7:56 PM

UST Computer Science Department welcomes students from local high schools for a Connection Event

When?

  • April 26th from 1 pm to 5 pm
Where?
  • University of Saint Thomas, O’Shaugnessy Science Hall, 4th floor
What else do I need to know?
  • Come to network with students and faculty and learn about classes, jobs, research and most importantly, skills and technology here at UST
  • It’s free!

Schedule:
The afternoon will be split into three seminars. Every person will be put into a small group that will rotate through these sessions. In the middle of the afternoon, we will have a social hour where you can hear about classes, jobs and research from students and professors.

Seminar 1: Computer Build
Seminar 2: Unity Game development
Seminar 3: Oculus VR demonstration
Also see a 3D printer in action!

Registration:
High School students register here.
The first 15 students to register will be sent more information via email.
