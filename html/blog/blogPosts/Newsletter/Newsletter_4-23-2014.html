The Happenings

At our club meeting yesterday, we went over all the events coming up for the rest of the semester as well as recorded nominations for next year's executive board. Check out the nominations at the bottom of the newsletter. If you want to run, or nominate someone for a position, just send me an email. Elections will be going on next club meeting! To vote you must be an undergraduate who has attended at least 3 club meetings.

Candidates please prepare a speech for the election.

Service Opportunity

Join us to make a difference and complete your service hours! USG is hosting a Feed My Starving Children meal packing event. We will be making a difference on Saturday, May 3, 2014 by busing UST undergraduate student volunteers to the Feed My Starving Children Chanhassen location to pack food. We will be providing a quick breakfast before the event at 9:30am in Scooter's and will be back by 2:30pm. To register, please sign up here.

High School Event

Anyone wishing to get easy volunteer hours this Saturday, register on the club website or email Amanda @mari2295 regarding when you can help. Remember that there will be opportunities to help outside of the actual date on Saturday too if you absolutely can't attend. If you know of any high schoolers who would be interested, the flyer for the event is attached for you to give to them. Thanks for your help!
