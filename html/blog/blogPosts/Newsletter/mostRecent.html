We will be taking applications for a Social Chair for the upcoming year. The Social Chair is responsible for heading the Social Committee and thus getting social events planned. This is not an elected posiotion, the executive board will conduct interviews and choose a candidate. If this is of any interest to you, shoot me an email and we'll set up an interview.
